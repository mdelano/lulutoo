About Lulu Posh Hair

Lulu Posh Hair is located in the beautiful, seacoast town of North Hampton New Hampshire. The Lulu Posh Hair team has been styling and serving the seacoast for the past 3 years Additionally, they've been traveling the country designing and teaching event hair trends for special occasions, advanced hair-cutting systems and customer service tools for success. Margaret Delano, founder of Lulu Posh Hair, has built a team and a large client base as a result of her dedication to knowledge of the profession and continuous education in the beauty industry. Her educated team brings quality hair design to their guests. Lulu Posh Hair is passionate about customer service excellence. We create an experience that takes you away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Creating a sanctuary to "just be" is what we stand for.

To reveal and inspire beauty. To support and uplift self image. To generously educate.

The Lulu Posh Product Line

We feature products by Eufora, an all organic solution to hair care. There are several key points of difference in Eufora product formulations. Most notably is Eufora's use of pharmaceutical grade Aloe Vera Gel, as the base for formulations. This unique ingredient provides a performance benefit unlike anything found in salons today. Eufora is the only salon professional product line to provide the legendary healing benefits of Aloe Vera in an optimum quality concentration. Eufora utilizes the most advanced botanical ingredients available that have scientifically been proven to benefit the hair, scalp and skin. Eufora uses pure essential oils to fragrance all products, for an aromatherapy experience. Eufora's unique blends of botanical, vitamin and other non-carcinogenic ingredients deliver performance that will exceed your highest expectations.
